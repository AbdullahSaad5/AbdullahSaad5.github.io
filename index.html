About

Who / what

I'm a web and AI engineer based in Islamabad, currently building AI products at DTCForce. For 3+ years I've shipped production web apps across the MERN stack, Next.js, and React Native, from automated car-financing platforms to full dealership systems to real-time 3D configurators. Lately my focus is where LLMs meet production systems: AI agents that take real actions, action-selection across hundreds of integrations, and keeping a non-deterministic model inside rails that don't move. I write up the hard parts so other people don't have to learn them the painful way.
